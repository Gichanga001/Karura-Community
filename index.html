Our Roots
A Brief History

Karura Community Football Club was founded in 2004.

The three stars on our crest represent the levels we have climbed to reach our current position.

We currently compete in the FKF Central Region League.

We are a Christ-like football club engaging the world through sports.

Our slogan, "a ball and a life", means we don't just play football — we offer opportunities to young people through mentorship, education, and guidance for life after the field.

Frequently Asked
FAQ
Interested players can attend our open training sessions held every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday morning at Muchatha Ground. Bring your boots and water. Our coaching staff will assess you during training and you'll receive feedback within a week. Contact us at info@karurafc.co.ke for more details.
Yes! The Karura FC Youth Academy was established in 2017 and caters to players aged 8–18 years. We offer structured coaching programmes across U10, U13, U16, and U18 age groups. The academy trains on Saturday mornings at our ground. Enrolment is open at the start of each season.
All Karura Community FC home matches are played at Muchatha Staium, off Banana-Raini Rd, Muchatha, Kiambu County. Admission for league matches is free to the public. Check our social media for match schedules.
We offer a range of sponsorship packages to suit all budgets — from jersey branding to ground signage and digital promotion. Fill in the Sponsoership form or Email our marketing team at sponsors@karurafc.co.ke or call +254 712 345 678. We'd love to have you on board!
Absolutely. KCFC relies heavily on the generosity of community volunteers in areas such as matchday operations, kit management, social media, photography, coaching assistance, and youth mentorship. If you're interested, reach out via email and tell us how you'd like to get involved.
Karura Community FC currently competes in the FKF Central Regional League — one of the key regional competitions under the Football Kenya Federation (FKF) structure. We earned promotion to this level in the 2020/21 season.
No, currently we do not have a women's team. We hope to establish one in the future as the club continues to grow.
